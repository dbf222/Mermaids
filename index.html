About Us

Founded in 2020, Mermaids & Tadpoles’ offer family oriented swim lesson, tailored to the individual need of each “Mermaid” or “Tadpole”!

I have over 30 years of swimming experience ranging from an All-American swimmer, age group coach, lifeguard and swim lessons instructor which has taught me to tailor each lesson to meet the child where they are, and go at their own pace.

Through one on one instruction, I create trust with the family and their children working one on one to get them comfortable and feel safe in the water!
